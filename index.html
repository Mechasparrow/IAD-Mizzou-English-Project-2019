About

Hello and welcome to the website on Internet Addiction Disorder and Social Media Addiction.

As technology and the internet grows and changes, it serves as a tool to connect with others and achieve more than what can be done with human ability alone. Just like any tool, it can be misused/abused leading to the potential harm of ourselves and others.

Here you will find pages talking about why Internet addiction is a problem, how it can be solved/alleviated, and resources for those wanting to learn more or seeking help.

The website is for those who want to learn more about IAD, those who may be struggling with IAD, or those who might know of someone who struggles with IAD.
